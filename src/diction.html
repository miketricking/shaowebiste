Dictionary

Dictionary

This dictionary covers all of the terms frequently used in classes and offers guidance on pronunciation. All terms are in Mandarin and are written in Pinyin, including the tones.

How to pronounce these letters in Mandarin:
X = sh
C = ts (ce = tse sounds like "tsuh")
Z = ds (zi = dsi sounds like "dzuh")
Zh = j (zhuanti = "jew en ti")
