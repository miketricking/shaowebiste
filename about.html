About

We've got what you need!

Shaolin Kung Fu originates from the Shaolin Temple in Henan, China. Whilst there are many styles of martial arts which make use of the Shaolin name, we train in the style practiced in the temple by the Shaolin monks. The almost unique experience of our instructors allows us to incorporate aspects of traditional Shaolin Kung Fu practiced by folk masters on Songshan mountain which are no longer practiced in the temple. Shaolin Kung Fu’s long history has seen it influenced by many martial arts practitioners from all over China and making it a vast system of self-defence. Our syllabus is therefore wide ranging and includes: traditional fist and weapon forms, each of which is its own sub-style or part of a sub-style, containing many practical applications; acrobatics which the Shaolin monks are famous for; Chin Na, which covers grappling and joint locks; Sanda, also known as Chinese kick boxing. Shaolin is also well known for its practitioner’s fitness, flexibility and body conditioning; our training has been designed to allow quick progress in these areas whilst ensuring safe and balanced development. Whilst many exercise routines focus almost entirely on the larger muscles we include strengthening of joints matched with carefully planned introduction of techniques to avoid injury. The Southampton Shaolin Temple Kung Fu Club was set up over a decade ago by Shifu Yan Xin to spread Shaolin culture and martial arts, and forms part of his KungFu.Life (link: http://www.kungfu.life/index.php) group. You can find some of his instructional videos here (link: https://www.youtube.com/user/masteryanxin)
