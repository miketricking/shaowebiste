Lessons

Shaolin Lessons in Southampton

We hold a number of classes every week, each focussing on different aspects of kung fu



  • Forms and Fitness
  • Sanda
  • General Kung Fu
  • Advance Kung Fu
  • Master Seminars

This class focusses on improving students’ forms and fitness with fundamental kung fu exercises. Forms, or Taolu, are a sequence of predetermined movements which teach self-defence applications and principles. They also develop strength, endurance and agility, and so form a fundamental part of kung fu training.


This class focusses on Sanda, also known as Sanshou or Chinese kick boxing. Sanda is a combat sport which focuses on punches, kicks and throws. This class also covers defensive techniques and basic fighting principles which fall outside sports sparring. Whilst Sanda is a competitive sport, not everyone wants to take part in free sparring and so this is optional. The club owns protective equipment, gloves and pads, however some people choose to bring their own training equipment.


This is our main training session where we practice all aspects of Shaolin Kung Fu including forms, Sanda, acrobatics, Chin Na and basic drills. Acrobatic training includes moves which the Shaolin monks are famous for such as the xuen feng jiao (tornado kick) and xuanzi (butterfly kick). Chin Na covers grappling, joint locks and escapes which are of use in self defence.


This class is for those with a strong grasp of the basics of kung fu. This class involves practicing more advanced techniques and forms, and requires a greater level of fitness.


Throughout the year the club hosts training seminars run by Shifu Yan Xin. These are a great opportunity to learn new skills and improve your level of kung fu.

Prices

As our main class is on a Saturday, for the purposes of fees we run a Saturday-Friday week. If you attend a Saturday class you may attend all of the other classes that week for free. If you miss the Saturday class midweek classes are charged individually.
