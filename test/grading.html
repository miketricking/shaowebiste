gradings & membership

How our grading works

Traditional ways of measuring progress in Shaolin Kung Fu cannot be reasonably applied to study outside of the temple. In the UK we face other commitments and cannot train for 5 hours every single day. We have developed a grading system which recognises our student’s achievements, offers a guided training syllabus and can accommodate many years of practice. The grading system is designed to reflect your martial arts journey, with faster progress made at the earlier tiers and increasingly rigorous requirements for the later grades.

We also recognise that some people may never excel at certain areas of kung fu; as such our grades are broken into four tracks, technique, application, fitness and flexibility, and mobility. Forms then act as the gateway to the next tier. The first two tiers require completing all the tracks as these represent the fundamentals of Shaolin Kung Fu. For the following tiers students must pass at least three of the four tracks and grade one or more forms at varying levels of skill. The grid below shows how the core forms are distributed over the different tiers. There are also supplemental forms which fall outside of the core syllabus.

Success in a grading is represented by adding to wrist bands modelled on mala, Buddhist prayer beads. The beads are coloured for each aspect of the grading system passed, thus each person’s mala will represent their personal kung fu journey and their individual talents.

Membership

Membership of the Southampton Shaolin Temple Kung Fu Club offers a number of benefits. When you first become a member you will receive a club uniform consisting of a t-shirt and trousers which have been designed for kung fu training.

Membership also provides you with membership of the British Council for Chinese Martial Arts, allowing you to take part in their competitions, and attend their events and courses. Membership costs go towards the purchasing of the uniform, BCCMA membership and the ongoing running of the club.

Finally membership allows you access to the members area of our website which contains a number of guides on stances, forms and vocabulary and a detailed breakdown of our syllabus.
